What is the Hollywood Peel?

Hollywood Peel is a treatment that offers incredible facial rejuvenation results. This service is performed with the highest and most innovative laser technology, known as Q-Switched. It is applicable for most skin phototypes, obtaining as a result a luminous, uniform and fresh skin with just one session. This aesthetic treatment has become the favorite of celebrities such as Angelina Jolie and the Kardashians. The fanaticism of the latter for this technique is well known, a reason that has given rise to its name. And, without a doubt, it is a fabulous technique that offers one of the best results in terms of facial rejuvenation. The Hollywood Peel treatment consists of first applying a thin layer of activated carbon on the face, letting it act so that it is absorbed, then working with the specific pointer for this type of treatment, emitting laser light on the carbon, which is vaporized. removing all dead cells instantly. Secondly, the process is repeated, this time without a mask, raising the temperature, thus favoring the stimulation of collagen.

How many Hollywood Peel sessions are necessary?

How many Hollywood Peel sessions are necessary? As it is an aesthetic treatment that is not invasive and rather depends on your skin type and specific needs, the number of sessions will be personalized. A Hollywood Peel treatment for a teenager with acne problems is not the same as for more mature skin. On the other hand, it will also depend on what result you want to obtain and even on your facial cleansing habits, diet, health, etc. Although in general, at least 4 Hollywood Peel sessions are usually required, performed at intervals of several weeks and even monthly. The results are usually seen from the first session, although as we have already mentioned, the most general recommendation is one session every month for four months in order to obtain a lasting effect for approximately one year. How effective is the Hollywood Peel?

How effective is the Hollywood Peel? Well, you only have to look at the complexion of the Kardashians and Angelina Jolie to give us an idea of ​​​​the results. Of course, as we indicated before, the Hollywood Peel also depends on the number of sessions and on each skin in particular. However, you will notice a young and luminous skin, totally improved. What should be done after a Hollywood Peel treatment?

The Hollywood Peel is such a good technique that no further treatment is usually required after it is done. What is important is to start or continue a skin moisturizing routine, with creams that contain vitamin A or even solid Vaseline. Being a type of peeling, it is also necessary to use a sunscreen with SPF greater than 50, especially if you are prone to skin pigmentation.
