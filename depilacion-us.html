Diode Laser Hair Removal

Diode Laser Hair Removal is an effective, safe, painless, fast technology, suitable for all skin and hair types, that can be applied all year round, and that achieves visible results from the first session, both for women and men who for reasons aesthetic or health professionals want permanent hair removal.
With Laser Hair Removal, it is possible to eliminate between 80% - 90% of unwanted hair.

"Visible results from the first session.
The best option for beautiful, soft and sensual skin."

Benefits of diode laser

With laser hair removal you get smooth and delicate skin every day of the year. You no longer have to worry about having to wax yourself, or pass the blade for that special occasion. You can go to the gym or the pool, or wear any type of clothing with complete peace of mind. In the greatest number of cases, a treatment of 6 to 8 sessions every two months will be enough to say goodbye to unwanted hair.

Before laser hair removal

Good hydration is necessary to avoid irritation and discomfort in the areas that we are going to wax.
Avoid sun exposure at least 5 days before the session, so that the melanin of the skin is not active and the laser acts selectively on the melanin of the hair and the light is not absorbed by the skin.
Do not remove the hair from the root (neither wax nor machine) at least four weeks before the session. This way we achieve that the hair is active (anagen phase) and the session is effective.

After laser hair removal

Moisturize and regenerate the skin for at least a week (a gel with aloe vera is a good option).
Avoid exposing the skin to the sun for 5 days after the session.
Do not pull out the hair from the root. The body will expel the destroyed hair and it will begin to fall more or less 15 days after the session. If desired, it can be shaved.

Frequently asked questions

Doubts about laser hair removal

How many sessions are necessary?

The number of sessions will depend on various factors such as the area to be treated, the type of skin and hair, age, sex, hormonal factors, etc. As a general rule, between 6-8 are necessary for body areas in the case of women, and between 8 – 10 in the case of men.

Is it really painless?

Yes. Thanks to its refrigerated head, it is possible to minimize the discomfort of the treatment, in some cases feeling heat and small pricks.

How long are the sessions?

The sweeping system allows faster sessions, since they are carried out by dragging the head along the surface of the skin. This system also avoids leaving areas without waxing. To know the duration, choose the area on the photo in the rates section

How much time elapses between sessions?

The usual thing is between one to two months for body areas. For facial, every month and a half to two months. This interval increases as the hair weakens and the amount decreases.

Is the treatment safe?

Yes. The new SHR technology minimizes the risk of burns, since a homogeneous distribution of energy is achieved.

Do I need pre-session preparation?

The sessions are done without hair, so we recommend shaving the area 2 or 3 days before to avoid skin irritations. The skin must be hydrated and clean, without traces of creams, oils, deodorant, makeup, etc.

Can I wax between sessions?

Yes, as long as a system that does not extract the hair from the root is used. We recommend shaving with a blade.

Can I sunbathe or get UV rays during the treatment?

Direct sun exposure of the treated area should be avoided for 3 or 4 days before and after the session. It is also not advisable to use UVA rays or self-tanners.

Can I wax if I take any kind of medication?

There are certain types of medications that are contraindicated in laser hair removal, such as: RACOUTAN, SINTRON, CORTICOIDS, CORTISONE, ANTIBIOTICS. However, if you are taking any other type of medication, in case of doubt you should consult before carrying out the session.

What if I am pregnant or breastfeeding?

In these cases, laser hair removal is not recommended.
