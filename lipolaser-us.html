Lipolaser

¿Qué es la lipolaser?

Body contouring with Laser Lipo Indianapolis differs from traditional liposuction. It is not invasive.
Laser Lipo also serves as a painless treatment. It uses low-level lasers to target areas of the body that don't respond to regular exercise and diet.
Laser Lipo neutralizes fat cells under the skin. This causes them to leak their content into the surrounding tissues. This allows the lymphatic system to drain toxic waste.
Low level laser therapy also increases cell metabolism. It further stimulates collagen production. This makes the skin look firmer and more vibrant.
The laser targets fat in many areas of the body, including the chin, abdomen, upper back, and lower back. It also benefits the waist and buttocks, as well as the arms and underarms.
Laser Lipo Indianapolis targets areas of the body that consistent diet and exercise have failed to improve.
